Top Rappers Rocking with face tattoos:

Lil Wayne:

Lil Wayne, the Grammy-winning rap icon, is known for his extensive tattoo collection. With intricate designs covering his face and neck, he took it a step further by adding head tattoos. From teardrops to symbols representing his hometown, New Orleans, Lil Wayne's head tattoos add to his unique persona.

Post malone:

Post Malone, a multi-platinum artist, is recognized for his fusion of rap and melodic elements. He sports numerous tattoos, including a barbed wire pattern across his forehead. While controversial, this head tattoo has become a recognizable symbol of his unconventional style.

21 savage

With his distinctive voice and raw lyrics, 21 Savage's head tattoo is an intricate map of his life journey. The tattoo features various symbols representing his experiences and personal growth. It serves as a powerful visual representation of his resilience and authenticity.
