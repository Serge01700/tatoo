Symbolic tattoos and their meanings.

Tree of Life:

This symbol represents strength, growth and the connection between heaven and earth. It is often associated with rebirth, family, wisdom and protection.

The Eye of Horus symbol:

A symbol of protection, mental clarity and healing, often associated with ancient Egypt. It is considered a powerful talisman to ward off evil and bring good fortune
